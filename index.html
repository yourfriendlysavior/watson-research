Early Watson

Watson was created in 2007 by the IBM research team for the purpose of competing on the television game show, "Jeprody" It later went on to beat the two of the best Jeprody players in the world, proving to not only be able to unwind complex saements back to their question orgins, but able to do it quickly. After the Jeprody game, the IBM team took Watson and upgraded him to what he is today, and they are still ading to his capabilities.
