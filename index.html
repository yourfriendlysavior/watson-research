Watson is a problem solving AI software created by IBM and named after their founder, Thomas J. Watson. It is a supercomupiter that processes at a rate of 80 teraflops with access to over 90 servers with a combined data score of over 200 million pages of information. Combine that with 6 million logic rules to process each choice against.

Watson was created in 2007 by the IBM research team for the purpose of competing on the television game show, "Jeprody" It later went on to beat the two best Jeprody players in the world.
